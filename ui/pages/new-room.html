The problem

Yet another meeting is steering into the wrong direction. Two people have a heated discussion while the other four wish we could just proceed to the next thing in the agenda.

The idea

Whole team joins an online "room" anonymously. Each participant can either thumbs-up or thumbs-down the current course of the meeting. Total score of thumb-downs is displayed, and it's up for a team to have some ground rules how to deal with that. Do we stop and change topic after there are 3 thumb-downs on the screen?

Give a room a meaningful name, click create, then share the URL with other participants.
